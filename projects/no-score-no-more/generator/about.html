Our Story

At No Score No More your credit score doesn’t matter. You may ask yourself “How can they get me approved without a credit score?”. “How” is with over 30 years of experience of getting car buyers into their dream cars, regardless of their credit status. Our experience in working with lenders across the country has helped us identify the key factors outside of your credit score that help us find financing options that meet our customers’ needs. With the assistance of No Score No More, car buyers can go to a dealership with confidence, and without an inquiry on their credit report. Connect now with an agent.
